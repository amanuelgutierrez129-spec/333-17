About SQC PRO

Established professional financial consulting institution rooted in Northern Ireland

Our Corporate Strength

SQC PRO is a formal financial information consulting service company located in Nottingham, Northern Ireland. We have a senior professional team with rich financial industry experience, focusing on global financial market analysis, industry information sorting, investment risk assessment and customized financial consulting planning.

We adhere to the service concept of integrity, professionalism and efficiency, strictly abide by industry norms and market rules, provide one-stop high-end financial information consulting services for individual investors, enterprise institutions and cross-border financial practitioners, and win wide recognition and trust from customers all over the world.

With complete information collection channels and mature data analysis system, we keep track of real-time financial dynamics, sort out valuable core information for customers, and effectively avoid market risks and optimize financial layout.

Development History

Founded in 2012, SQC PRO started with regional financial consulting services in Northern Ireland. After 12 years of development, we have expanded our business to Europe, Asia, North America and other regions, and established long-term cooperative relationships with more than 8,000 clients worldwide.
